Orthography

Ո, Օ

The following orthographic and phonetic rules govern the usage of the letters Ո ո, Օ օ in East Armenian.

Ո ո

Ո ո occurs

  • ոʋoinitial position of words
  • ոomedial and final position position of words

In initial position of words pronouncedʋoas in English volt

որդի

ʋoɾtʰi

son

որս

ʋoɾs

hunting

ոտք

ʋotkʰ

foot

In medial and final position of words is pronounced o as in the English folk

չորս

ʧʰoɾs

four

բոլոր

boloɾ

all

Կարո

kaɾo

Karo (male name)

Within compound and derived words is pronounced ʋo after vowels, o after consonants

արջաորս

aɾʧʰaʋoɾs

bear hunting

ամենաորակյալ

amenaʋoɾakjal

most qualified

ձկնորս

ʣᵊknoɾs

fisherman

անորոշ

anoɾoʃ

indefinite

Exeptions

The initial ո is pronounced oin the pronouns

ով

ov

who

ովքեր

ovkʰeɾ

who (pl.)

Nouns of foreign origin starting with vo are spelled with վ rather than the regular ո in initial position

Վոլգա

volga

Volgaa

վոլտ

volt

volt

ապօրինի

apoɾini

կեսօր

անօգուտ

Proverbs

havə tʰevov‿e tʰᵊṙʧʰum, maɾtʰn – azgakanov

Birds fly with their wings, humans with their kin.

voɾtʰin hoɾn‿e kʰaʃum, aχʧʰikə – moɾə

A son takes after his father, a daughter after her mother.

(Your child) grew, your pain grew.
